How does a Vendor register with RegSoft.com?
Registering with RegSoft.com is easy and fast. Vendors must simply complete the Product Submission form on this website (Click Here for the Vendor Submission Form). Vendors should have a product that is currently, or is soon to be, available for sale. Just click here to connect to RegSoft.com's Vendor Login Area. Then, click in the area where it says, "If you are a new vendor". This will take you to our secure server, and RegSoft.com will walk you through filling out two forms. However, if you have not already so, you may wish to print and review RegSoft.com's official Agreement before starting this process. All personal information is held in the strictest confidence.

The software information is added to RegSoft.com's Software Library. The submission is made in real time and will be ready for sale immediately upon completion of the form. At that time, the vendor will be assigned an Account ID number and a Product ID number. These are unique numbers to help identify the vendor and the product so our vendors can easily update their information or product information online. Once the information for the product is processed, the vendor can inform potential users that registration of the product may be completed in an online, secure environment at RegSoft.com.

RegSoft.com can provide the vendor with a button or banner to place on the page that contains the exact URL to where people can register your software at RegSoft.com. The vendor may select a banner or button from a number of attractive options (click here to view some samples of available vendor buttons and bars). When the registration is completed successfully, we will notify you via E-Mail that a new user has registered your software.

What's the policy on advertising your 800 number in a magazine?
Our toll-free number should only be published as ORDERS ONLY. Users tend to call a toll-free number with a question or two and it can tie up the lines quickly depending on the circulation of the magazine. It is best to provide an alternate number as well for technical support, even if they reach a machine. Let us know before the ad runs so we can prepare and be sure the price we quote will match that listed in the magazine.

I was thinking out having RegSoft.com generating my serial numbers for me. What are my options?

We can support the following methods to generate a key for your product.

1) a VB COM object that we can call in IIS (make sure it's Active X and that the DLL references the Active Server Pages Object [asp.dll]) All input paramaters have to be defined as "variants".

2) a console program that can take 1 or 2 parameters, and write the key back out to stdout. It cannot have any forms, and has to exit by itself. for example:

C:>genkey.exe "Bill Smith"
867-DSD-565-FDD

You can pass it any type of paramater as well, and it has to write just the reg code to stdout.


3) Logic to create a key that we can write in our web scripting language PHP.

4) We call a URL on your site to get the key. for example:

http://www.yourserver.com/genkey.asp?name=rob%zino

and your code just displays the key back to the browser.
