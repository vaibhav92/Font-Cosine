RegSoft.com strives to provide vendors and authors superior flexibility in shareware registration and distribution. There are many options for vendors, all of which help vendor’s provide dependable transactions and customer satisfaction in ways that work with their corporate environment. RegSoft.com can be involved as much as little as desired by the vendor. For example, RegSoft.com handle all the registration while the vendor simply collects the monthly checks, or the vendor may choose be 100% involved in handling the registration process. If none of the scenarios below meet your needs, kindly contact support@RegSoft.com , and RegSoft.com will work with each vendor or firm on an individual basis to customize a registration program that is complementary to a vendor’s existing systems.

For vendors participating in the E-Commerce Payment Solutions, registration of software can be handled in a number of different ways. Below is a brief list of some likely registration & distribution scenarios:


Typically Encountered Scenarios in Registration and Distribution Options:


  • A customized registration can be arranged so that RegSoft.com handles all the registration process while the vendor/author simply collects a monthly check. Or, RegSoft.com can arrange for the vendor/author to be completely involved in each of the stages of the registration process.

  • For products that have just one registration key, a vendor/author can supply RegSoft.com with the key, and RegSoft.com will then forward it to registered users once payment is verified.

  • For products that have multiple registration keys, a vendor can supply us with a month’s worth of code, and we forward the applicable codes to registered users once payment is verified.

  • In special cases such as products that have a unique key based on a user’s name or products that ship a full version once the user registers, RegSoft.com can work out a customized arrangement. For example, RegSoft.com can notify the vendor/author when a user registers, and the vendor/author may then generate the key or ship the user a full version.

  • If a vendor chooses to have RegSoft.com handle the purchase arrangement, but not the formal process of registering the product, RegSoft.com will send a vendor a confirmation notice any time a user registers and the vendor may forward the user the necessary registration information.
